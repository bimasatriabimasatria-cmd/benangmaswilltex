Company History

Established in 2005, TextileCorp started as a small mill and has now grown into one of the largest yarn and fabric manufacturers nationwide. We are fully committed to maintaining export quality standards.


Our vision is to become a pioneer in the eco-friendly textile industry. We produce various types of yarn ranging from cotton, polyester, to rayon with strict quality control.
